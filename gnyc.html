Who Are We?

We are Green New York City (GNYC), a orginization working to turn the very urban and poluted NYC into a more nature friendly enviroment with the help of those living in it.

What Do We Do?

We allow peple to design different landscapes like parks and gardens that contributes to keeping a safe and healthy evironment. We also look for volunteers and people to donate as much as they can, because even the smallest ammount can make a huge difference.
